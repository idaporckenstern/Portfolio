Rubiks Cube Solver

I had been looking for a project, and I saw a Rubiks Cube Solver online that I thought was really cool and I decided to give it a try. I am still at the very early stages in this project so there is still a lot to do.



Source Code

Project Details

In order to save in costs, I am using servos to turn the Rubiks cube. My goal is to automate the entire process by having a camera read the colors on the cube in order to solve it. I am using OpenCV to read the colors and give the input to the Kociemba algorithm. This then gives a solution that is at most 20 turns. I have made the code to detect the colors and I am currently designing the chassis to hold the camera, cube, and servos.

To Do

Once I have a chassis, I need to calibrate the OpenCV code to properly store the color configuration so it can be plugged into the solver. I still haven't decided if I am going to have a servo at each face or if I will just use four and rotate the cube to get the bottom and the top. The biggest issue I am running into design wise is how I can have the camera read the imput without being blocked by the arms. My current plan is to have two concentric pipes that will make a rack and pinion gear with the inner pipe being the rack. A pinion is on the outside that raises the inner pipe up and down. The cube gets raised up to the camera to show a face, then switches sides and shows the camera again.
